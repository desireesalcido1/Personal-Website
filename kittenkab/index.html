KittenKab Mobile App Design

This design was for a class project where I was tasked with improving the user experience of an app for a client named KittenKab. KittenKab is a company that allows users to order kittens to play with, with their ultimate goal being those kittens being adopted. Their goal was to make the adoption process easier for their busy users so that they would see an increase in kitten adoptions. KittenKab found that users thought their current adoption process was inefficient and time consuming, which led to a decrease in kitten adoptions. I was hired to add a feature to their app that would allow users to adopt kittens from the comfort of their own home which would increase the number of kitten adoptions
